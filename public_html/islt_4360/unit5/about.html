About The Ethics Game

The Ethics Game is an experimental and exploratory game created to educate players on the importance of major ethical decisions. It features the five major ethical theories and determines on which moral grounds players make their decisions.

About the Creator

Clover is a student at the University of Missouri studying digital storytelling. She is currently a game designer for the SISLT department. With the guidance of Alek Willsey, graduate philosophy student, the Ethics Game became a possibility with the vision to bring ethics and responsibility to the world of gamers.
